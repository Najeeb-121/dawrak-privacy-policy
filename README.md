# Privacy Policy for Dawrak (دورك)

**Last updated: March 21, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how Najeeb Alsheikh ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the **Dawrak** mobile application ("the App").

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Phone number** — used to create and verify your account
- **Username** — chosen by you, displayed to other users
- **Full name** — used to identify you within the app

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Device token (FCM)** — used to send push notifications to your device
- **Usage data** — how you interact with associations, invitations, and payments within the app

### Information Shared by Other Users
- Other users may invite you to financial associations using your username
- Other users may add you as a friend using your username or phone number

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Create and manage your account
- Allow you to create, join, and manage rotating savings groups (Jamiya/جمعية)
- Send you notifications about invitations, friend requests, and association updates
- Enable communication features between users (friend system, invitations)
- Improve the performance and functionality of the App

---

## 3. How We Store Your Information

Your data is stored securely using **Google Firebase** (Firestore and Firebase Authentication), which is hosted on Google's servers. Firebase complies with industry-standard security practices.

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.
